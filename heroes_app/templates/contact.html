15+ Questions Answered

What is Compassion International?

Compassion International is a global child development organization that partners with local churches to support children from vulnerable backgrounds through education, healthcare, mentorship, spiritual guidance, and life empowerment programs.

What is RGC Heroes of Faith?

RGC Heroes of Faith is a church partner working together with Compassion International to nurture, mentor, and empower children and youth within the community through holistic child development programs.

Who benefits from the Compassion program?

Children from humble and vulnerable backgrounds benefit from the program. Some children are enrolled from as early as three years old, while others are supported even before birth through caregiver support initiatives.

What is the main goal of the program?

The main goal is to empower children and youth to become self-reliant, responsible, confident, and impactful members of society through holistic development and mentorship.

At what age do beneficiaries exit the program?

Most beneficiaries graduate from the Compassion program at the age of 22 years after receiving years of support, mentorship, education, and empowerment training.

How does child sponsorship work?

Sponsors contribute financially to support a child’s educational, medical, emotional, and spiritual needs. The support is administered through the local church partner to ensure children receive holistic care.

Can sponsors communicate with children?

Yes. Sponsors are encouraged to write letters, send messages, and build encouraging relationships with the children they support.

How are donations used?

Donations help provide school support, healthcare services, meals, mentorship programs, vocational training, youth camps, and community development initiatives.

Can I support without sponsoring a child directly?

Yes. You can support community projects, youth empowerment initiatives, outreach activities, educational resources, or volunteer your skills and time.

What programs are offered to children?

Children receive educational support, healthcare services, mentorship, Bible teaching, talent development, emotional support, and participation in social events and camps.

Are healthcare services provided?

Yes. Beneficiaries receive medical checkups, health education, and support for maintaining proper physical wellbeing and hygiene.

What opportunities are available for youth?

Youths participate in leadership training, vocational skills programs, mentorship sessions, career guidance, talent nurturing, and empowerment camps.

What are camp meetings and outings?

Camp meetings and outings bring together beneficiaries from different regions for learning, mentorship, fellowship, team-building, and personal growth experiences.

Are birthday and Christmas celebrations important?

Yes. These celebrations help children feel loved, valued, and appreciated while creating joyful memories and strengthening community relationships.

How can someone get involved?

You can get involved through sponsorship, volunteering, mentorship, donations, prayers, or partnering with RGC Heroes of Faith in community outreach programs.
